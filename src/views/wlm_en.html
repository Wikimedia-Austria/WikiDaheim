Wiki Loves Monuments in Austria

Wiki Loves Monuments in Austria is integral part of WikiDaheim and the Austrian contribution to the international photo contest Wiki Loves Monuments (WLM). Pictures of cultural heritage monuments (marked in red in wikidaheim.at) from the overall WikiDaheim contest automatically take part in the local WLM contest and will be awarded with a special prize. Best 10 pictures of cultural heritage monuments uploaded between September 7th and October 6th will be nominated also for the international contest. If you want your image to be eligible for WLM international, you have to wait with your upload until September.

On Wiki Loves Monuments in Österreich you can find the inventory of all cultural heritage monuments in Austria, that are in the scope of this photo contest.

See general page Photo contest WikiDaheim for rules and prizes.
