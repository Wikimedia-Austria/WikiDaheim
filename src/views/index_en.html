Welcome to WikiDaheim!

WikiDaheim is a project for Wikimedia projects like Wikipedia, Wikimedia Commons and Wikidata, as well as RegiowikiAT, that focuses on the villages and areas of Austria. There are still many aspects of life in Austria that are undocumented and need your help in documenting them by taking pictures or writing articles.

Competition

The next competition takes place between July 1st and October 6th, 2024 (24:00 CEST). During this timespan you can upload your media to the competition. You can already take photos in advance, topics will be similar to those of the past years. The winning pictures of previous years can be viewed on the page dedicated to the competition.
