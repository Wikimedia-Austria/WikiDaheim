Tag des Denkmals

Tag des Denkmals is an event as part of the European Heritage Days organized in Austria by the Austrian Bundesdenkmalamt (BDA). Each year, the BDA publishes a list of participating objects about highlights of the Austrian cultural landscape at Tag des Denkmals. Wikipedia cooperates with BDA in that Wikipedia authors mirror these lists to Wikipedia, where they build the foundation of our common photo contest.

Photo contest on Tag des Denkmals

Photos of buildings and other objects, presented at the Tag des Denkmals on September 24th, 2023 (always the last sunday in September), can be uploaded from July 1st till October, 8th, 2023 (24:00 CEST), and nominated for the photo contest. You will find all the participating objects at tagdesdenkmals.at. Tag des Denkmals often allows photos of interiors that are otherwise not publicly accessible. This photo contest is part of WikiDaheim, which aims to illustrate and describe Austrian municipalities.

The same rules as for WikiDaheim do apply.

See general page Photo contest WikiDaheim for rules and prizes.
