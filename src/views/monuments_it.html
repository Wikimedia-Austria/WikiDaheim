This topic covers all monuments protected by the Federal Monuments Office in Austria, currently encompassing almost 40,000 buildings.

What should I look out for?

Of particular interest for protected buildings are the current condition and unique features, which also makes new pictures of already photographed buildings worthwile.

Additionally, when uploading pictures of details of a building it is very helpful to add a description that describes the detail shown in the picture as well. Categories can also be very useful in order to make it easier to find your pictures.

You can find an overview of pictures uploaded so far on Wikimedia Commons.
